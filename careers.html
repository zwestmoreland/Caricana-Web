Caricana Enterprises Career Opportunities

Caricana Enterprises prides itself in being a premier partner for businesses in the Caribbean, wishing to source products and services internationally. In so doing it is imperative that we attract the right individuals to properly represent our culture, vision and services offered.

We are first and foremost, a service oriented organization, faithfully catering to the needs of our customers, ensuring that their needs are met professionally. We offer the right individuals, the unique opportunity to build a successful business representing a host of established and respected brands.

Currently, we are looking for representatives/agents in the various islands that will promote and earn present and potential customer’s business.

At Caricana Enterprises, you’ll not only be shaping the ideals that will dictate the way we work and live, but you will be designing your own future.

Available positions:

Executive Sales Representative:

We are quickly becoming the largest product based distributor in the Caribbean. To enhance this achievement we intend appointing key agents to represent each of the Islands.

Therefore, we are looking for experienced representatives/agents in the various islands that will promote and boost present and potential business.

This job requires effective and cordial management of key accounts as assigned or initiated by you. You have to be comfortable cold calling and dealing with top level businessmen. Your goal is to listen and clearly identify their needs, so we may quote and supply accordingly. In addition, you will be responsible to promote and sell our wide range of products to the respective industries.

Qualifications:

High School diploma, computer literacy, Internet access and E-mail, Valid drivers License and vehicle

Skills:

-Strong business connections
-People person,
-Must be a self starter
-Responsible,
-Excellent communication skills,
-Proven successful sales experience,
-Ability to work without direct supervision
-A good handle of the economy
-Ability to identify new opportunities
-Understanding and implementing the concept of total service
-Ability to travel or conduct business in the other Islands is an asset.
-Second language is an asset.

If you qualify or know someone who does, please have them apply.

We provide complete support and excellent commissions and incentives to the right individuals.
